Fated Match

Tired of spending your days all by yourself? Struggling to actually connect with someone? Well do we have a solution for you! Welcome to Fated Match 2.0! All you have to do is express your opinion of five statements. Each response option correlates with a number 1-5 which is then used to calculate your compatability. Good luck and answer honestly! 1 = Strongly Disagree, 2 = Disagree, 3 = Neither agree or disagree, 4 = Agree, 5 = Strongly agree
